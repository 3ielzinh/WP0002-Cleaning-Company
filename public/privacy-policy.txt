SparClean Cleaning Services LLC (“SparClean,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects the privacy of our customers, prospective customers, website visitors, and other individuals who communicate with us.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, protect, and disclose personal information when you visit www.sparcleanbr.com, request an estimate, schedule or receive cleaning services, communicate with us by telephone, email, text message, social media, or online form, or otherwise interact with SparClean.
By using our website or voluntarily providing information to us, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy does not create a contractual right beyond the rights provided by applicable law.

The information we collect depends on how you interact with SparClean and which services you request.
A. Contact and Identification Information
We may collect:
* Your first and last name;
* Email address;
* Telephone or mobile phone number;
* Billing address;
* Cleaning service address;
* City, ZIP code, and general service area;
* Preferred method of communication; and
* Information contained in messages, inquiries, forms, or service requests you send to us.
B. Service and Property Information
To prepare an estimate, schedule a cleaning, or perform services at your property, we may collect:

* Number of bedrooms, bathrooms, rooms, offices, or other areas;
* Requested cleaning frequency;
* Requested services, add-ons, and special instructions;
* Information concerning pets, occupants, parking, alarms, gates, or building access;
* Entry instructions, lockbox information, door codes, or gate codes that you voluntarily provide;
* Information about surfaces, materials, furnishings, appliances, or areas requiring special care;
* Information about allergies, sensitivities, fragrance preferences, or product restrictions that you voluntarily provide;

* Preparing or confirming an estimate;
* Recording pre-existing damage or unusual conditions;

* Marketing SparClean’s services when separate permission has been obtained.
We will not intentionally publish photographs or videos that identify you, your address, personal documents, family members, or other private information without appropriate permission.
Permission to use images for marketing may be withdrawn for future use by contacting us. Withdrawal does not necessarily require removal of materials that were lawfully published or produced before we received the request, where removal is not reasonably possible.
D. Payment and Transaction Information
When you make a payment, we may receive or maintain information such as:

* Payment date;
* Payment method;
* Invoice number;
* Transaction confirmation;
* Billing status; and
* Limited payment-related details supplied by a financial institution or payment provider.
